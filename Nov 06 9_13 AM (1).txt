46. Comment on the following pair of sentences:

The next statement is false.
The preceeding statement is true.
This pair creates a paradox known as the Liar Paradox. If the first statement is true, then the next statement (which says the first is false) would be false, contradicting itself. Conversely, if the first statement is false, then the next statement is true, which again contradicts itself.
